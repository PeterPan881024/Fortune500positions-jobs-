Target Audience
🔍 Job Seekers: Precisely locate target positions at Fortune 500 companies and gain advance understanding of job requirements.

📚 Students: Research the distribution of positions across industries and plan career development paths.

🔬 Workplace Researchers: Analyze recruitment trends of Fortune 500 companies and changes in industry demand.

💼 HR Professionals: Refer to the recruitment standards and position settings of leading enterprises.

Data Advantages

Bilingual Comparison: All job information includes both Chinese and English versions, suitable for job seekers at home and abroad.

Ready-to-Use: Direct links to recruitment pages are provided, eliminating the need for additional searches.

Comprehensive Information: Covers core recruitment elements such as job responsibilities and work locations.

Authoritative and Reliable: Based on the Fortune Global 500 list, ensuring the credibility of enterprise qualifications.

Contribution & Updates

Disclaimer

This dataset is for learning and reference purposes only and does not constitute employment recommendations.

Recruitment information has timeliness; please verify the validity of positions on the company's official website before applying.

In case of issues related to corporate privacy or infringement, please contact the maintainers to delete the relevant content.
